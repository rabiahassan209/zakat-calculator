What is Zakat?

Zakat is one of the Five Pillars of Islam. It is a mandatory form of charity that every Muslim who owns wealth above the Nisab threshold must pay annually. The word Zakat means purification and growth in Arabic.

Allah says in the Quran: "Take from their wealth a charity by which you purify and cause them increase." (Quran 9:103)

About This Calculator

This free Islamic Zakat Calculator was built to help Muslims around the world easily calculate their annual Zakat obligation. It covers all major categories of zakatable wealth including gold, silver, cash, savings, business assets, property, and investments.

Features

  • 10 currency options including PKR, USD, SAR, EUR, GBP and more
  • Gold and silver Zakat calculation with current prices
  • Cash and bank savings calculation
  • Property and rental income calculation
  • Business assets and investments
  • Automatic Nisab threshold calculation
  • Deductions for debts and liabilities
  • Full wealth breakdown with individual Zakat amounts

Islamic Principles Used

  • Nisab is based on 85g of gold or 595g of silver (lower value used)
  • Zakat rate is 2.5% of net zakatable wealth
  • Liabilities and debts are deducted before calculation
  • Property used for personal residence is not zakatable
  • Vehicles for personal use are not zakatable
